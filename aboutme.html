About Me

Helloooo and welcome to my website where I share foods that I want to try when I start traveling.

Before I get into why I love food so much, a little bit about myself. I am a college student at the wonderful University of Michigan. I was born in India and have lived in India, Canada, and several states within the United States. Raised in a very traditional family, I have grown to love my culture and the foods that have originated from India. However, that was just the beginning. Today, the food culture today is exploding. From traditional cuisines to fusions, many new dishes are created and shared. With options comes indecisiveness and is exactly why I decided to research and make a list of foods I want to try in the near future.

I hope that my list is appealing to you as well and wish you a very memorable food journey when you travel or spend time with your loved ones.
